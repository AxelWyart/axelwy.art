About me

I am a Paris-based multidisciplinary designer with over 10 years of experience building products & experiences.

I currently lead Design at Laiye, where we push the boundaries of AI, automation and customer support to help businesses work smarter and more efficiently. At Laiye I do hands-on work and lead international design teams working on our products and marketing efforts.

Before Laiye, I was Head of Design at Mindsay where I had the opportunity to build and manage the design team, its processes and foundations, which led to its acquisition by Laiye.

Prior to Mindsay I worked for an innovation studio called Blank as an interaction designer. During that time I was fortunate to build products and experiences for a range of clients such as Redbull, Galeries Lafayette, Bpifrance, Malakoff Humanis in projects of various forms, from websites to physical IoT objects and even interactive installations..
